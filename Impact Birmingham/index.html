Investing in Our Community

Church of the Highlands is proud to host two annual fundraisers—a golf tournament each spring and Race to the Heights 5K every fall—that resource local Impact United Appeal Fund non-profit organizations. Proceeds from each event directly benefit these selected organizations, enabling them to make a difference in our community.
